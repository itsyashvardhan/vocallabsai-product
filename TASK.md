Use SWOT / 7Ps / Porter's Five Forces or any framework at your disposal.

## HOW WILL THIS BE EVALUATED


### MOVES FORWARD

- Real, non-obvious observation 
- Specific screens/copy/flows
- Tradeoff thinking
- Clear observation→problem→solution chain
- Prioritisation logic

### DOESN'T MOVE FORWARD

- Generic feedback ("improve UX", "add gamification")
- All 5 in one area
- Features without an observed problem
- Obvious signs the app wasn't used
- Polished but shallow
